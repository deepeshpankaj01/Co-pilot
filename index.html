The Problem

Challenges in Modern
Clinical Decision Making

India's healthcare system faces a silent crisis — overwhelmed doctors, scattered data, and AI tools that act as untrustworthy black boxes.

01
Real Problem in Healthcare
Overwhelmed Doctors, No Time
  • 5 million+ die every year from medical errors & delayed diagnosis in India
  • Lung, heart & liver diseases are difficult to diagnose early
  • Doctors handle 100+ patients daily in major hospitals like AIIMS
  • Average consultation time is only ~5 minutes per patient
  • Must review symptoms, history, reports & scans simultaneously in 5 minutes
02
Difficulty in Comparing Treatment Outcomes
No Evidence at Decision Time
  • Doctors need to know which treatment worked best in similar past cases
  • Need real-time access to success rates and recovery timelines
  • Current AI gives predictions without explanation
  • Doctors cannot trust opaque algorithmic recommendations
  • Complex patient data cannot be analyzed in a 5-minute window
03
Missed Patterns & Diagnostic Errors
India's Massive Disease Burden
  • High cases of TB, heart disease, kidney disease & liver disorders
  • No system to instantly find similar patient cases across databases
  • AI tools act as black boxes without clear clinical evidence
  • No system automatically analyzes treatment success patterns

The 5-Step AI Pipeline

A clinically-grounded pipeline combining AI analysis, historical patient intelligence, and treatment outcome data to support faster, safer clinical decisions.

Step One

Patient Data Collection

The system collects complete patient medical information to build a structured clinical profile. Every data point contributes to more accurate matching and analysis.

Step Two

Step Three — Core Innovation

Similar Patient Search

The AI searches large medical databases (MIMIC-IV, NIH, ICMR) to find patients with similar symptoms, reports & diagnoses. For each match: confirmed diagnosis, treatment used, recovery outcome, and time taken.
